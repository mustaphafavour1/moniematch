Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026. This policy explains how MonieMatch Technologies Ltd collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Who We Are

MonieMatch Technologies Ltd ("MonieMatch", "we", "us", or "our") is a Nigerian technology company operating the MonieMatch platform — an investment matching service that connects small business owners with everyday mini-investors. Our registered address is Lagos, Nigeria.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@moniematch.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Create an account: name, email address, username, and password.
  • Set up a profile: city, state, occupation, investment preferences, or business details.
  • List a business: business name, category, description, funding needs, revenue range, and links to social profiles or catalogues.
  • Send messages: content of in-app messages and deal communications.
  • Submit reports: monthly revenue figures, narratives, and challenges submitted by business owners.
  • Upload files: profile photos, business photos, and legal documents stored in our secure buckets.
  • Contact us: any information you include in support or contact form messages.

We also collect information automatically, including IP address, device type, browser, pages visited, and usage patterns.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account and profile.
  • Power our matching algorithm — calculating compatibility scores between investors and businesses.
  • Facilitate deal negotiations, agreements, and monthly reporting.
  • Send transactional emails (account verification, password resets, deal notifications).
  • Improve our platform, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

4. Sharing Your Information

When you engage with another user on MonieMatch (as an investor viewing a business or a business receiving investor offers), profile information relevant to the match is shared between matched parties. This is a core function of the platform.

We use the following trusted third-party services who may process your data under their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — our database, authentication, and storage provider (GDPR-compliant, servers in Frankfurt).
  • Vercel — our hosting and deployment platform.
  • Anthropic Claude — AI assistant features on the platform.
  • Zoho Mail — email delivery for transactional messages.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and profile data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or to resolve disputes.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent to optional data processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@moniematch.com.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your session. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings, but this may log you out of the platform.

8. Security

We take security seriously. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Database access is restricted by Row Level Security (RLS) policies so users can only access their own data. File storage buckets use access-controlled policies to prevent unauthorised access.

However, no system is perfectly secure. Please protect your account password and report any suspected security issues to security@moniematch.com.

9. Children

MonieMatch is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and notify users of material changes via email. Continued use of MonieMatch after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at privacy@moniematch.com or write to MonieMatch Technologies Ltd, Lagos, Nigeria.
